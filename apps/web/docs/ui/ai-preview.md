# AI Product Preview Page

## Goal
Allow clinics to preview their logo on physiotherapy products before ordering.

---

## Components

### 1. LogoUploader
- Upload clinic logo
- PNG / JPG
- Max 5MB

---

### 2. ProductSelector
- Select product type:
  - Bag
  - Sheet
  - Pad
  - Full Pack

---

### 3. ColorSelector
- Available colors:
  - Black
  - Navy
  - Burgundy
  - Coffee
  - White

---

### 4. VariantSelector
- Size
- GSM
- Handle type

---

### 5. PreviewCard
- Show mockup image
- Show logo on product

---

### 6. AI Recommendation Box
- Suggest best color
- Suggest print color
- Show contrast score

---

### 7. PriceCard
- Estimated price
- Quantity input
- Discount rules

---

### 8. CTA
- "Continue to Order"
- "Save Design"

---

## UX Rule
- Mobile first
- Simple Persian UI
- Maximum 3 clicks to preview
